Best of CES Asia Award 2017 参选说明

1评选简述
亚洲消费电子展最佳产品奖（Best of CES Asia Award）是亚洲消费电子展展会官方奖项。
奖项设立的目的是鼓励科技产品的创新，推动科技产品提供优异用户体验。
2017年Best of CES Asia Award由亚洲消费电子展媒体合作伙伴，中国领先的科技门户网站中关村在线负责评选。
2参选资格
参选厂商需为CES Asia 2017的注册参展商。未正式取得展位的厂商不得参与评选。
参选产品需为已开发完成且需为2017年5月11日前公开发布或销售的产品。
参选技术或服务必须已经正式发布而且在2017年底前可以提供给用户。
参选产品、技术或服务必须在CES Asia 2017上正式展示。
3奖项设置

Best of CES Asia Award 2017的评选原则是创新、应用价值和市场潜力。
本次评选将按照类别颁出的奖项，分为以下四类：

Best of CES Asia Winner获奖产品和技术：

获奖产品/技术需要有独特的创新价值，包括在技术、设计、功能或应用等领域的改进，并且具备实用价值。
在所有提名产品中，获奖产品至少在某个方面具备独特的优势。
获奖产品的市场表现与业内口碑也将成为评选的参考依据。
每个奖项分类有一至两个获奖者，可能空缺。

Best of CES Asia Finalist提名产品和技术

提名产品需在设计、技术、功能、应用等方面具备独有的特色与优势。
提名产品应当代表了其所在产品领域的最新发展趋势，具备充分的应用价值和市场潜力。
每个奖项分类将选出不多于4款提名产品，可能空缺。

Best of CES Asia Editor’s Choice编辑选择奖

编辑选择产品是参与评审的编辑从技术、设计、功能、应用等角度，在展会现场所有展品中选择出的产品，代表所有评审编辑的共同观点。

Best of CES Asia People’s Choice 用户选择奖

用户选择奖由互联网用户在提名产品的展示页面投票产生，获得最多票数的产品/技术奖颁发用户选择奖。

5评选流程
时间 事项 说明
03.20-05.19 申报 线上申报参选产品阶段
05.22-06.02 初审 ZOL评审团对线上申报的产品进行初审评分（线上产品申报持续开放中）

6.09 公布评选结果 线上与展会现场同步发布提名及获奖产品名单
6申报须知

1. 每个厂商申报产品数量原则上不得超过5个, 每个产品只能选择一个类别。

2. 同一产品不得由多家厂商共同申报。

3. 申报产品不得侵犯他人权益。

4. 参选厂商上传的产品相关资料，评选方将以予以保密。

Best of CES Asia Award 2017 Application Rule

1About the award

The Best of CES Asia Awards, presented by ZOL, is the official awards program of the 2017 International CES Asia.
The award is focused on breakthrough products and technologies that encourage innovation and evolution across 15 categories. In addition to category-specific awards, ZOL will also present the Best of the Best and People’s Choice Award. Winners will be selected by an expert panel of judges from ZOL, a leading technology portal site in China and media partner of the 2017 CES Asia. For full details and to submit products for consideration, visit http://ces.zol.com.cn/best-of-ces-asia

2Qualification

Applicant

Applicants must be the exhibitors of CES Asia 2016. Application will not be accepted without a valid booth number of CES Asia 2016.

Product nomination
Nominated product must be complete product released before May 11, 2016.
Nominated technology/service must be available for end users before the end of 2016.
The product/technology/service must be displayed on the show booth of CES Asia 2016.
3Award Setting and selection criteria

There are three main assessment criteria for the Best of CES Asia Ward: Innovation, Application Value and Market Potential

Award Setting.
In each category, there will be Award Winner and Award Finalists. For People’s Choice Award and Editor’s Choice Award, we only present one award winner respectively.

Winners.
Winners will be awarded to at most two products (service/technology) in each category. Awarded winner should have unique value of innovation, including but not limited in technology, design, function and application improvements, and the winner should also be practical in daily applications. In all the nominated products, the winner should have at least one extinct advantage in above mentioned area. The market performance and user feedback will also be considered for the candidates, if available

Finalists.
At most four finalists will be awarded in each category in nominated products. Finalist products should have unique feature and advantage in design, technology, feature or applications in its category, they are representing the leading trends respectively in its category

Best of the Best.
There will be one Best of the Best award. The device, service or technology in any category that stands clearly above the rest, judged based on its innovation and design quality by our editor team.

People’s Choice Award.
One product with most vote on ZOL.com.cn will be named People’s Choice. All nominated products will be open for vote during CES Asia open time.

5Award selection procedure

The award will be selected by the following the schedule.

20th March C 19th May Online Submission Exhibitors submit nominated product online
22nd May C 2nd Jun Primary Assessment Review the recieved documents.
7th Jun C 8th Jun Final Assessment Review and discussion of the final result. Award winners may be required to present more information.
8th Jun Announcement Award Result for each category will be announced online, and a ceremony of the award will be held on ZOL booth
9th Jun People’s Choice Vote Vote online for the People’s Choice Award by visitors of the show
6Regulations

Each applicant should submit no more than five products under normal situation.

Each product may only enter one category.

One product can be submitted by only one exhibitor.

Nominated product should not violate the rights of other parties.

All information and documents submitted will be treated as classified.
